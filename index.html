The Royals Offense

The Royals offense has been led by a surging Bobby Witt Jr. and a re-surging Salvador Perez. While the leadoff batter has been a work in progress, the 2, 3, and 4 hitters (Witt Jr, Vinnie Pasquantino, and Perez) have been consistent and dominant. The Royals pitching has been a tale of two staffs. The starting pitching has been impressive and produced two all-stars this year in Cole Ragans and Seth Lugo.
